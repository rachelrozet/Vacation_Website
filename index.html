Top 3 Food Items You Should Try at Magic Kingdom

Build Your Eating-Itinerary for Your Next Disney Trip

November 11, 2024

With Cinderella's castle at its center, Magic Kingdom is the heart of Orlando's Walt Disney World.

Whether you're a frequent Disney visiter or a once-in-a-lifetime park-goer, make sure to taste these goodies before saying goodbye to Mickey!

  • Dole Whip at Aloha Isle

    A creamy, pineapple-flavored soft serve dessert, available both on its own or as a pineapple-juice float

  • Gaston's Cinnamon Roll at Gaston's Tavern

    Endulge in a treat as big as the character himself, with a gooey cinnamon center perfect for a comfort breakfast or a pull-apart snack

  • Waffle Sandwich at Sleepy Hollow Refreshments

    Pair a fluffy, golden brown waffle with a selection of sweet or savory fillings, from fruit and Nutella to fried chicken and hot honey
